Tank Man – Jeff Widener (1989)

One of the most iconic photographs was taken on the 5th of June 1989. The man was persistent blocking tanks’ path and Jeff thought that there would be one more tragic death. Fortunately, the man was whisked away, but Widener managed to capture the moment that would later become a symbol of resistance to unjust regimes in different parts of our planet.

The Burning Monk - Malcolm Browne (1963)
